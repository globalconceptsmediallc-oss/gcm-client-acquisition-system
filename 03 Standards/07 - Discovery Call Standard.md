# Discovery Call Standard

Version: 1.0

Status: Active

Purpose:

Define the standard for conducting discovery conversations that determine whether a qualified prospect and Global Concepts Media are an appropriate long-term business partnership.

---

# Principle

Discovery comes before recommendation.

A business cannot receive an effective recommendation until its goals, challenges, opportunities, and constraints are clearly understood.

The objective of a discovery call is understanding—not selling.

---

# Why It Matters

Without a structured discovery process:

- Recommendations are based on assumptions.
- Client expectations become misaligned.
- Trust develops more slowly.
- Solutions address symptoms instead of root causes.
- Close rates and long-term retention decline.

A disciplined discovery process creates clarity for both the prospect and the agency before any recommendations are made.

---

# Standard

Every qualified prospect should participate in a structured discovery conversation before receiving recommendations or proposals.

The discovery call should seek to understand:

- The business and how it creates value
- Primary products or services
- Target customers
- Business goals
- Current marketing efforts
- Sales process
- Growth objectives
- Existing challenges
- Success measurements
- Budget considerations
- Decision-making process
- Timeline for implementation

The conversation should focus on listening, asking thoughtful questions, and validating understanding before discussing solutions.

Recommendations should only be made after sufficient information has been gathered.

---

# Decision Framework

Before concluding the discovery call, ask:

Do we clearly understand the business?

Do we understand the primary growth objectives?

Have the major obstacles been identified?

Can measurable opportunities be explained?

Is Global Concepts Media positioned to create meaningful value?

Would accepting this client align with our Ideal Client Standard?

If any answer is no, additional discovery should occur before advancing the opportunity.

---

# Measurement

Track:

- Discovery calls completed
- Discovery-to-proposal conversion rate
- Discovery-to-client conversion rate
- Average discovery duration
- Qualification accuracy
- Client fit score
- Average sales cycle after discovery
- Client retention following discovery
- Revenue generated from discovery conversations

---

# Continuous Improvement

The discovery process should improve through:

- Closed business reviews
- Lost opportunity analysis
- Client onboarding feedback
- Sales performance analysis
- Team coaching
- Industry experience
- Emerging business trends

Every discovery conversation should contribute to improving future qualification and recommendation quality.
